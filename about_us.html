HOW WE WORK

IEEE PESIT student branch provides an environment to foster learning and developing real word skills. In keeping with these principles, events targeted towards various skill sets and domains are organised. It is devoted to it's aim of instilling a scientific temperament in the student community whilst contributing towards the greater good of the global engineering community
